Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our web design services

How much does a website cost in Sydney?

Website costs vary based on complexity and features. Our basic business websites start at $3,000-$5,000, while more complex sites with custom functionality range from $7,000-$25,000+. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your specific needs. Contact us for a free consultation and custom quote.

How long does it take to build a website?

Typical timeline is 4-8 weeks for a standard business website, depending on the scope and how quickly you provide content and feedback. E-commerce sites and more complex projects may take 8-12 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during the planning phase.

Will my website work on mobile phones?

Absolutely. Every website we build is fully responsive, meaning it automatically adapts to look perfect on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. Mobile optimization is a core part of our design process, not an afterthought.

Can I update the website myself?

Yes. We build most websites on WordPress, which makes it easy to update text, images, and content without coding knowledge. We provide training and documentation to help you manage your site confidently. For more complex updates, our support team is always available.

Do you provide website hosting?

Yes, we can arrange quality hosting as part of our service, or work with your existing hosting provider. We recommend Australian-based hosting for faster local performance and better SEO results for Sydney businesses.

Will my website rank on Google?

We build SEO best practices into every website, giving you a strong technical foundation. However, ranking well requires ongoing SEO work including content creation, link building, and optimization. We offer comprehensive SEO services to help you achieve and maintain strong rankings.

What happens after the website launches?

We provide ongoing support through our Website Care packages. This includes security updates, backups, technical support, and maintenance. We're also available for future enhancements, content updates, and digital marketing services as your business grows.

Do you redesign existing websites?

Yes, we frequently redesign outdated websites to improve their appearance, performance, and conversion rates. We can migrate your existing content to a new, modern platform while improving SEO and user experience.
